# Finance Committee Charter

Status: Draft

## Purpose

Define the finance committee's governance responsibilities for stewardship, oversight, reporting, and financial controls.

## Responsibilities

- Budget development and monitoring.
- Monthly financial review.
- Policy recommendation and maintenance.
- Oversight of financial controls.
- Reporting to church leadership and business meetings.
