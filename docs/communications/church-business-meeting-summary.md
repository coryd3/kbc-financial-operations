# Church Business Meeting Summary

Status: Draft

## Plain-Language Summary

KBC is reviewing how our financial work is organized because the Treasurer position is currently in transition. Linda McConville has agreed to help as interim Treasurer so the church can maintain continuity while we review the longer-term structure.

This review is not about blaming any person or committee. It is about making sure our financial system is clear, accountable, transferable, and sustainable.

Over time, the Treasurer role has included both financial accountability and many day-to-day bookkeeping tasks. The church is considering whether those responsibilities should be more clearly separated so the Treasurer, Finance Committee, and any bookkeeping support each have a clear role.

## Areas Being Reviewed

- Treasurer responsibilities.
- Day-to-day bookkeeping and financial administration.
- Finance Committee monthly review.
- Reimbursement and spending authority.
- Credit/debit card or controlled payment practices.
- Financial software.
- Documentation and procedures.
- Any approval or vote required by church bylaws or policy.

## Current Direction

The goal is to build a financial operations system that supports good stewardship, clear reporting, and continuity when volunteers or staff change roles.

No final policy, role, compensation, or software decision should be treated as approved until it has gone through the appropriate committee review and church approval process.

Needs Review: This summary should be reviewed by Pastor, Deacons, Finance Committee, and Personnel Committee before presentation to the congregation.
