# Church Business Meeting Summary

Status: Draft

## Summary

The Treasurer position is currently in transition. Linda McConville has agreed to help as interim Treasurer so the church can maintain continuity while we take time to review the best long-term structure.

This is not about blaming any person. We are thankful for those who have served and helped carry this work. The goal now is to make sure our financial process is clear, sustainable, and easier to pass on in the future.

The Personnel Committee is reviewing role descriptions, possible bookkeeping support, application materials, and any staffing or compensation questions.

The Finance Committee is reviewing the financial side of the process, including software, reimbursement practices, spending approval, monthly review, and the best way to divide responsibilities between the Treasurer, any bookkeeping support, and the committee.

The church is also beginning to evaluate financial software options that may help with giving records, bookkeeping, reports, and monthly review. No final software decision has been made.

Any future recommendation will come back through the proper committee process. If a church vote is needed for a role, budget item, software decision, or policy change, that will be brought to the church before final action is taken.

Needs Review: This summary should be reviewed by Pastor, Deacons, Finance Committee, and Personnel Committee before presentation to the congregation.
